What is GDMUN?
At this point, do we even need to ask? Some of us have been here before, arguing, negotiating, surviving on caffeine and last minute research. It has been four years since we dusted off our placards post lockdown and somehow, each edition has only gotten crazier (in the best way possible). The excitement never really faded and now, we’re here again , back to where it all begins. The brainstorming , the elaborate discussions, the never ending check lists , all leading up to yet another edition "GDMUN 2025" Will it be bigger? Probably. Bolder? Most definitely. More fun? Without a doubt.
